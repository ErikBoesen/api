About

Yalies is a website that provides data on the Yale student body. It combines data from Yale Face Book and Yale Directory, and contributes additional analysis to provide as much useful information as possible.

Remove your information

All data used by Yalies is drawn from the Yale Face Book and Yale Directory. If you wish to remove any information about yourself, you may visit the Yale SIS and remove whatever information you desire. The information will promptly disappear from Yalies as well.

Source Code

You may access the source code of this website on GitHub.

Author

Yalies is developed by Erik Boesen. For questions, comments, bug reports, or suggestions, contact erik.boesen@yale.edu.
