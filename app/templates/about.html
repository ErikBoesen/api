About

Yalies is a website that provides data on the Yale student body. It combines data from Yale Face Book, Yale Directory, and other sources, and contributes additional analysis to provide as much useful information as possible.

Of interest to developers, and unlike official Yale data sources, Yalies also provides a powerful and flexible API to make fetching student info a breeze. No need to parse the Yale Face Book HTML for your projects.

For questions, comments, bug reports, or suggestions, contact erik.boesen@yale.edu.

Remove your information

All data used by Yalies is drawn from the Yale Face Book and Yale Directory. If you wish to remove any information about yourself, you may visit the Yale SIS and remove whatever information you desire. The information will promptly disappear from Yalies as well.
