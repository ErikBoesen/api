Remove your information

Yalies only uses data that is already publicly available to Yale students, drawing from the Yale Face Book and Yale Directory. No data is used that is not already available from those sources.

If you wish to remove any information about yourself from Yalies, you may do so through the Yale Face Book "Edit" page. Because Yalies only uses data that is already readily available from these sources, removed information will promptly disappear from Yalies as well.
